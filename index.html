The WPRC Ski Team is part of the Seven Springs Ski Resort which is in Champion PA.The Wprc ski team has eight to ten races each season, all of the runs count twoards points for making the state team.

The first week of the season is Christmas camp which lasts through December 26 to New Years Day, New years is only one session. Halfway through the camp the team has a Pot Luck which everyone goes to after practice. Everyone will bring a dish to the pot luck outside the WPRC training hut.

There is also a end of the year banquet which the whole team attends. There is food served in the Grand ballroom where the banquet is held. After everyone is done eating the kids can participate in games. Then the end of the year vollage video will comence. Then every Season new U18s graduate from the team and have to give a graduation speech. For the rest of the night the kids will mess around.
